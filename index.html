About Me

Hi! I'm Rob and I'd like to become a web developer because I love being able to challenge myself and I love solving problems using logic. I also enjoy the creative side of web development and being able to produce beautiful-looking sites.

For the last 10 years, I've been between 2 different careers: teaching English and copywriting. I worked mainly as a freelance writer and I taught English in Spain for some time. It was while learning SEO that I stumbled across web design. I learned HTML and CSS, and did some online courses to learn JavaScript. I now feel ready to take a full-time course in web development.

On this site, you'll find a range of projects I completed myself. One of the most challenging to make was the calculator. I spent a lot of time developing the functionality so that you can perform another operation on the result of the previous calculation. This site isn't fully responsive, but it is for most laptops and mobile devices.
